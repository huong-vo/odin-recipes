Vegan Salad

Descriptions

This vegan salad recipe is so easy to make, only taking at most 15 minutes to be ready. It can serve up to 4 people. The salad not only is fresh and appetizing with vibrant colors but contains many vitamins and minerals. It is a perfect side dish for any light or heavy entrée.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium carrots
  • 2 slicing cucumbers
  • 3 beetroots
  • 1 head red leaf lettuce
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lemon
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Wash and dry all the vegetables, with a salad spinner if possible.
  2. Peel off the carrots, cucumbers, and beetroots. Then, cut them into finger-long sticks.
  3. Vertically slice the lettuce heads into half and cut off their roots. Then, place them flat on a cutting board and horizontally slice them into 2-inch-long pieces.
  4. Cut each tomato in half. Then, place them flat on the cutting board and cut each half in half.
  5. Add all the veggies to a 16-quart bowl.
  6. Cut the lemon in half and squeeze it for 1 tablespoon lemon juice.
  7. In a medium bowl, whisk together the oil, lemon juice, and salt until well combined. Taste and adjust as needed.
  8. Pour the dressing over the veggie in the large bowl. Then, toss until everything is evenly coated.
  9. Add some raisins to the bowl if you want some sweetness.
  10. Enjoy your colorful salad!
