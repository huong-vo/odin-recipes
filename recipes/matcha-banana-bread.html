Matcha Banana Bread

Description

This moist and fluffy match banana bread is the result of numerous trials and fails modifying different recipes. It is sugar-free, salt-free, and customizable. It carries natural sweetness from bananas, moisture from butter and milk, crunchiness from nut, and unique grassy taste from matcha.

The recipe takes about 60 minutes, 10-15 minutes for prepping and 45-50 minutes for being ready to eat.

Ingredients

  • 5-6 large overripe bananas, depending on how sweet you want it to be
  • 1 ½ all-purpose flour or any flour of your choice
  • 1 tablespoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter
  • ⅛ cup unsalted butter or Cooking spray, depending on what you use to grease a loaf pan
  • ¼ cup unsweetened plain milk of your choice
  • 1-2 teaspoons matcha powder, depending on how much caffeine you can take in
  • ⅛ cup almonds or any nut of your choice

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Place the butter in a small bowl and melt it in a microwave for 30 seconds.
  3. In a medium bowl, mash the bananas with a fork until some chunky pieces are left.
  4. Crack the eggs directly into the bowl of bananas; make sure to remove eggshells if any. Beat them lightly with the fork. Pour ¼ melted butter into the same bowl. Whisk them until combined.
  5. Add the milk to the mixture and whisk together until combined. Set aside.
  6. Chop the almonds into smaller pieces by a knife or in a blender.
  7. In a large bowl, stir together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, matcha powder, and chopped almonds.
  8. Pour the banana mixture into the dry ingredients. Stir together until smooth. Set aside.
  9. Grease a 9-by-5-inch loaf pan with the remaining butter by a basting brush or with cooking spray.
  10. Pour and spread the batter evenly into the prepared pan.
  11. Place the pan into the oven. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Once the bread is done, take it out of the oven and let it cool in the pan for 5-7 minutes. Then, transfer it out of the pan to completely cool down.
  12. Slice the bread and enjoy it with your favorite yogurt or ice-cream.
